Pending Transactions

Pending transactions are transactions that have been deducted from your available credit but have not yet posted to your account.

Transactions may take up to three calendar days to post. Pending descriptions and amounts may differ once posted due to merchants sending authorization requests before the final purchase amount is known. This commonly occurs with hotel, restaurant, gas station or car rental transactions.

Transactions cannot be disputed until they post to your account.
