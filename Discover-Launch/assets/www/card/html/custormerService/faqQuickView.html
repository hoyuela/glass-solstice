Frequently Asked Questions

Quick View

  • What are quick view settings?
    Turning on your quick view settings allows you to view your Current Balance, Available Credit Line, and Rewards Balance without having to log into the Discover Mobile App. It provides you a quick and easy way to view a snapshot of your account on the go.
  • How do I enable this?
    Navigate to Profile & Settings in the App and select 'Quick View.' On the landing page, you'll see an overview of how it works and you just need to turn the switch to "On." Once it is enabled, you'll see a new button that says, "Quick view" on the login page of the app. You can either tap the icon or swipe directly on the page to view your account information quickly.
  • How can I disable quick view settings?
    Navigate to Profile & Settings in the App and select 'Quick View.' On the landing page, turn the switch to "Off." Once it is turned off, the Quick view button will no longer appear on your login page and you will not be able to swipe to reveal your account information.
    You can also call Web Support at 1-877-742-7822 if you need to disable quick view settings.
  • Can I enable quick view settings on more than one device?
    Yes. You can enable this setting on any device that you use to access the Discover Mobile App.
  • If I have quick view settings enabled on my device and it gets lost or stolen, how can I disable it?
    In the event that your device is lost or stolen, please call Web Support at 1-877-742-7822 to disable your quick view settings.
