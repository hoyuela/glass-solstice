Frequently Asked Questions

General

  • Is the Discover Mobile App as secure as Discover.com?
    The Discover Mobile App uses the same security standards as Discover.com. We ensure a high security level by never showing your account number, timing out each section after a period of inactivity, and by using your existing User ID and Password, which only you know.
  • What do I need to log into the Discover Mobile App?
    To log in, you can use the same user ID and password that you have set up at Discover.com. If you do not have a user ID and password, tap on "Register Now" on the login screen.
  • What are the options to remember my device for the Enhanced Account Security?
    Choose "Yes" if you are using a private device that you will frequently use to access your account. By doing so, you'll still benefit from heightened account security, but will receive fewer security question prompts when logging in. Choose "No" if you are on a public device or if it is a device that you share with others who are not authorized Cardmembers on your account.

Passcode

  • Why should I use Passcode to log in?
    Using Passcode makes your entry into the Discover app a faster process. You no longer have to enter your entire User ID and Password to access your account information. It’s secure and easy to set up and use.
  • How do I set up Passcode?
    For first time users, navigate to Profile & Settings from the navigation after logging in with your User ID and Password and select "Passcode." You will be prompted to choose your 4-digit sequence that will become your Passcode. For your security, you must follow the Passcode Guidelines when creating your Passcode. For example, you cannot use your date of birth for your Passcode.

    After choosing a valid Passcode, we will ask you to re-enter it and then you will receive a confirmation message. A confirmation will also be emailed to you if we have your email address registered with your account. The next time you launch the app you’ll be presented with the Passcode login screen for quicker access to your account.
  • How do I change my Passcode?
    If you have a Passcode set-up and would like to change it, follow these easy steps:
    • Navigate to Profile & Settings
    • Select Passcode
    • Select Change Passcode
    • Enter your current Passcode
    • Choose a new Passcode that follows the Passcode guidelines
    • Confirm your new Passcode
    You can complete this process at any time. If you have Passcode enabled on more than one device, your new Passcode will apply to all devices with Passcode enabled.
  • How do I disable my Passcode?
    If you have Passcode set-up and would like to disable it, follow these easy steps:
    • Navigate to Profile & Settings
    • Select Passcode
    • Select Disable Passcode
    • Enter your current Passcode
    You can disable your Passcode at any time. If you have Passcode enabled on more than one device, it will continue to be enabled on those devices until you disable it for each device.

    You can also call 1-877-742-7822 to have a customer service representative disable your Passcode.
  • Can I enable Passcode on all of my devices?
    You can enable Passcode on any device that has the most current Discover Mobile application.
  • Can I log onto someone else's device with my Passcode?
    For security purposes, you can only log on to devices that you have registered your Passcode with.
  • What if I lose my device that has Passcode enabled?
    To protect your account security, we advise that you call 1-877-742-7822 to disable Passcode on all your devices. This will prevent others from attempting to log into your account with your Passcode.
  • What happens if I forget my Passcode?
    On the Passcode login screen, you can select "Forgot Passcode." You will then be prompted to log in with your User ID and Password and create a new Passcode.
  • Can I still log in with my User ID and Password?
    Yes, you can log in with your User ID and Password at any time by clicking on “User ID Login” from the login page.
  • What happens if I'm trying to log in with Passcode and enter it incorrectly too many times?
    For security purposes, you will be locked out of the application after 3 failed attempts. You can call 1-877-742-7822 to talk to a customer service representative about unlocking your account.

Payments and Transactions

  • How do I make a payment via the app?
    From the left-hand navigation, under Payments, select "Make a Payment."
  • How often can I make payments through the mobile app?
    You can make payments every day with a maximum of 2 payments per day using different bank accounts.
  • How long do mobile payments take to post to an account?
    Payments received before 5:00 p.m. Eastern Time will be credited to your account as of that day. Payments received after 5:00 p.m. Eastern Time will be credited to your account as of the following day.
  • What if I want to see more than the last 5 payments?
    If you want to see the last 7 months of payments, you will need to login to Discover.com. Once you are logged in, select the "Manage Account" option from the navigation bar and click on "Payment History" under the Payments in the drop down menu.
  • Will I be late if I submit a mobile payment on the due date?
    Your payment will not be late if it is submitted by 5:00 p.m. Eastern Time on the date it is due.
  • Can I cancel enrollment in mobile payments?
    Canceling enrollment in mobile payments will cancel enrollment in online payments as well. You can cancel enrollment in online payments by deleting all the bank accounts you use for mobile payments.
    Note: Any online or mobile payments scheduled to post will also be cancelled at the same time the corresponding bank account is deleted.
  • Can I edit or cancel a pending payment through the mobile app?
    You can edit or cancel a pending payment, which is not an automatic payment, from the Pending Payments page. To access the Pending Payments page, select "Payments" from the slide-out menu, then select "Manage Payments" from the Payments sub-menu. From the Manage Payments page, select the "Pending" button to view your pending payments. From the Pending Payments page, you can select to either edit or cancel your payment. If you choose to edit the payment, you will be able to modify the amount, posting date, or bank account.
  • How many transactions can I view on the app?
    You can view up to 24 months of transactions from the left-hand navigation, under Account, select "Search Transactions".
  • Can I view my pending transactions on the app?
    Yes. From the left-hand navigation, under Account, select "Recent Activity."
  • How can I access my most recent statement from the mobile app?
    From the left hand navigation, under Account, select "Statements" to access your most recent statement.
  • Can I save my statements from my mobile device?
    Yes. When viewing your statement select the "Download" button to save your statement as a PDF.
  • Can I store and use multiple bank accounts on my card account?
    Yes. You may use and store up to 8 bank accounts within one Discover Card account.
  • Will my bank information be saved if I make a mobile payment?
    Your bank information will be saved as a convenience for future online and mobile payments.
  • Can I set up multiple payments in advance using two or more different bank accounts?
    Yes, you can set up multiple payments in advance using two or more different bank accounts.

Push and Text Alerts

  • What is a Push Alert?
    A push alert is a message sent from the Discover Mobile App to your mobile device informing you when new account information becomes available. Depending on your device settings, these alerts can display in the form of badges, banners, or alerts and be viewed again later on the "Push Alerts History" page. You will only receive alerts for which you have enrolled. These subscriptions can be updated through the "Manage Text and Push Alerts" page on the app. No fees are associated with Push Alerts.
  • How do I turn on/off Push Alerts?
    Below are the ways to manage your Push Alerts:

    -From the left-hand navigation in the Discover App, under Profile & Settings, select "Manage Text & Alerts." Then select the Push Alerts you would like to activate/deactivate and save your changes.

    -To turn on/off all Push Alerts, slide the "Enable Push Alerts" switch from ON to OFF and vice versa, on the “Manage Text & Alerts” page and save your changes.

    -Push Alerts also can be turned off/on via your mobile device settings. To turn on/off notifications on your device, go to the notification center in the device settings and locate the Discover Mobile App icon. Click the icon and a screen will open. Choose the option to turn off/on Push Alerts.

    Note: If you disable Discover Push Alerts from your mobile device settings you will not be able to change your Push Alerts settings in the Discover Mobile App until you turn the Discover Push Alerts back on in device settings.
  • I activated Push Alerts. Why have I not received any?
    Check to make sure Discover Push Alerts are enabled in your mobile device settings. To turn on notifications on your mobile device, go to the notification center in the device settings and locate the Discover Mobile App icon. Click the icon and a screen will open. Choose the option to turn on Push Alerts.
  • What is the difference between Text Alerts and Push Alerts?
    Text Messages are available for all devices with text message receiving capabilities while Push Alerts are sent directly through the Discover Mobile App.

    Note:Text charges vary by carrier and plan. Please contact your service provider to find out if your plan has fees for receiving text messages.
  • How do I enroll in Text Messages?
    To enroll in text messages, from the left-hand navigation in the Discover App, under Profile & Settings, select "Manage Text & Alerts" and choose the text message you would like to receive. Once you have chosen your alerts, you will be asked to verify or provide your mobile phone number and carrier information. Enter or verify the information and then save your settings. You will then receive an opt-in text message from "DISCOV (347268)." You must text back "YES" along with the last 4 digits of your account to complete your enrollment.

    You can also enroll in text messages on Discover.com under Account Profile and Preferences.
  • What Text Messages will Discover send me?
    You will only receive text messages for which you have enrolled. You can elect to enroll in a number of text message reminders including when statements are available, when your payment has posted, and when your payment is due.
  • Can I receive fraud alerts via Text Message?
    Yes. You can sign up to receive fraud alerts that will notify you when unusual activity is suspected in your account. To enroll, select "Manage Text & Alerts," under Profile & Settings, from the left-hand navigation in the Discover app and select the box next to "Fraud Alert."

    You can also sign up for this alert on discover.com under Account Profile and Preferences.
  • How do I stop receiving Text Messages from Discover?
    Below are the ways to manage your Text Alerts:

    • On the "Manage Text & Alerts" page in the Discover App, select the text messages you would like to activate/deactivate and save your changes.
    • To unsubscribe from all text messages, text "STOP ALL" to DISCOV (347268).
    • Update your text message enrollments on Discover.com via your Account Profile.
  • I signed up to receive several text alerts. Why have I not received any?
    You may need to confirm the mobile number and carrier are entered correctly on the "Manage Text & Alerts" page in the Discover App or under Account Profile and Preferences on Discover.com. You should also check to see if you have selected the text messages you would like to receive from the "Manage Text & Alerts" page and resend "YES" along with the last 4 digits of your accounts to DISCOV (347268) to ensure you are opted in for the alerts. If the above options still don't work, please call Discover web support at 1-877-742-7822 or contact your mobile carrier to make sure you are able to receive text messages.
  • Can I contact Discover Card via text message?
    Discover does not provide two-way communication with its customers through text messaging at this time. If you would like to stop receiving reminders, reply "STOP" from the most recent text message reminder you received. If you still continue to receive text messages after you have replied, please contact Discover web support at 1-877-742-7822
  • Are text message reminders secure?
    We will never include your password or full account information in the message.

Quick View

  • What are quick view settings?
    Turning on your quick view settings allows you to view your Current Balance and Available Credit Line without having to log into the Discover Mobile App. It provides you a quick and easy way to view a snapshot of your account on the go.
  • How do I enable this?
    Navigate to Profile & Settings in the App and select 'Quick View.' On the landing page, you'll see an overview of how it works and you just need to turn the switch to "On." Once it is enabled, you'll see a new button that says, "Quick view" on the login page of the app. You can either tap the icon or swipe directly on the page to view your account information quickly.
  • How can I disable quick view settings?
    Navigate to Profile & Settings in the App and select 'Quick View.' On the landing page, turn the switch to "Off." Once it is turned off, the Quick view button will no longer appear on your login page and you will not be able to swipe to reveal your account information.
    You can also call Web Support at 1-877-742-7822 if you need to disable quick view settings.
  • Can I enable quick view settings on more than one device?
    Yes. You can enable this setting on any device that you use to access the Discover Mobile App.
  • If I have quick view settings enabled on my device and it gets lost or stolen, how can I disable it?
    In the event that your device is lost or stolen, please call Web Support at 1-877-742-7822 to disable your quick view settings.

Travel

  • What is 24/7 Travel Assistance?
    24/7 Travel Assistance provides you with a wide range of free travel assistance benefits while you are traveling. You can access the emergency services 24/7 by calling 1-800-DISCOVER (1-800-347-2683) or 1-801-902-3100 if you are outside the U.S.
  • What benefits are included with 24/7 Travel Assistance?
    Lost Luggage Tracking: We will help you locate lost luggage by staying in touch with your airline or other carrier, and provide regular status updates until your luggage is found.

    Emergency Cash Transfer: We will help you find the most easily obtainable funds and can locate ATMs and financial service institutions, or arrange to have cash wired or delivered while traveling.

    Passport and Travel Document Replacement: If your passport, identity cards or other papers necessary to return home are lost or stolen, our provider will offer assistance to replace them.

    Flight Rebooking/Ticket Replacement: If your tickets home are lost or stolen, or if illness or other emergency requires changing travel arrangements, our provider offers assistance replacing or rebooking your tickets.

    Log into Discover.com to view all your travel benefits.
  • Where is my Discover card accepted outside of the U.S.?
    Discover cards are currently accepted in multiple countries around the world through the Discover Network and our Network Partnerships: China Union Pay, JCB, and Diners Club International. Please visit our Country Acceptance Map on discover.com to select your travel destination from a full list of countries where Discover is accepted.
  • How do I contact the card service center when traveling outside of the U.S.?
    We are open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. While outside of the U.S., you can reach us by calling 001 plus 801-902-3100.
  • What should I do if my card is lost or stolen while traveling abroad?
    If your card is lost or stolen while traveling outside of the U.S. immediately contact the Customer Service Center in the U.S. by dialing 001 plus 801-902-3100.

    Line 2 includes:-The amount of the transaction in the foreign currency
