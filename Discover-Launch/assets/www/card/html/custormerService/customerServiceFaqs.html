Frequently Asked Questions

Security

  • Is Discover.com Mobile as secure as Discover.com?
    Discover.com Mobile uses the same security standards as Discover.com.We ensure a high security level by never showing your account number, timing out each session after a period of inactivity, and by using your existing User Id and Password, which only you know.

Log In Issues

  • What do I need to log in to Discover.com Mobile?
    To log in, use the same User Id and Password that you have set up at Discover.com.

Payments

  • How do I change accounts?
    To update your bank information, log in to Discover.com and access the Payments tab.
  • What if I want to see more than the last 5 payments?
    To see the last 7 months of payments, log in to Discover.com and access the Payments tab.

Transactions

  • How many transactions can I view on Discover.com Mobile?
    You can view the last 20 transactions posted to your account.
  • What if I want to see more than the last 20 transactions?
    To see up to 24 months of transactions, log in to Discover.com and click on the Recent activity and Statement links.

Quick View

  • What are quick view settings?
    Turning on your quick view settings allows you to view your Current Balance, Available Credit Line, and Rewards Balance without having to log into the Discover Mobile App. It provides you a quick and easy way to view a snapshot of your account on the go.
  • How do I enable this?
    Navigate to Profile & Settings in the App and select 'Quick View.' On the landing page, you'll see an overview of how it works and you just need to turn the switch to "On." Once it is enabled, you'll see a new button that says, "Quick view" on the login page of the app. You can either tap the icon or swipe directly on the page to view your account information quickly.
  • How can I disable quick view settings?
    Navigate to Profile & Settings in the App and select 'Quick View.' On the landing page, turn the switch to "Off." Once it is turned off, the Quick view button will no longer appear on your login page and you will not be able to swipe to reveal your account information.
    You can also call Web Support at 1-877-742-7822 if you need to disable quick view settings.
  • Can I enable quick view settings on more than one device?
    Yes. You can enable this setting on any device that you use to access the Discover Mobile App.
  • If I have quick view settings enabled on my device and it gets lost or stolen, how can I disable it?
    In the event that your device is lost or stolen, please call Web Support at 1-877-742-7822 to disable your quick view settings.

Passcode

  • Why should I use Passcode to log in?
    Using Passcode makes your entry into the Discover app a faster process. You no longer have to enter your entire User ID and Password to access your account information. It's secure and easy to set up and use.
  • How do I set up Passcode?
    For first time users, navigate to Profile & Settings from the navigation after logging in with your User ID and Password and select "Passcode." You will be prompted to choose your 4-digit sequence that will become your Passcode. For your security, you must follow the Passcode Guidelines when creating your Passcode. For example, you cannot use your date of birth for your Passcode.
    After choosing a valid Passcode, we will ask you to re-enter it and then you will receive a confirmation message. A confirmation will also be emailed to you if we have your email address registered with your account. The next time you launch the app you'll be presented with the Passcode login screen for quicker access to your account.
  • How do I change my Passcode?
    If you have a Passcode set-up and would like to change it, follow these easy steps:
    • Navigate to Profile & Settings
    • Select Passcode
    • Select Change Passcode
    • Enter your current Passcode
    • Choose a new Passcode that follows the Passcode guidelines
    • Confirm your new Passcode
    You can complete this process at any time. If you have Passcode enabled on more than one device, your new Passcode will apply to all devices with Passcode enabled.
  • How do I disable my Passcode?
    If you have Passcode set-up and would like to disable it, follow these easy steps:
    • Navigate to Profile & Settings
    • Select Passcode
    • Select Disable Passcode
    • Enter your current Passcode
    You can disable your Passcode at any time. If you have Passcode enabled on more than one device, it will continue to be enabled on those devices until you disable it for each device.
    You can also call 1-877-742-7822 to have a customer service representative disable your Passcode.
  • Can I enable Passcode on all of my devices?
    You can enable Passcode on any device that has the most current Discover Mobile application.
  • Can I log onto someone else's device with my Passcode?
    For security purposes, you can only log on to devices that you have registered your Passcode with.
  • What if I lose my device that has Passcode enabled?
    To protect your account security, we advise that you call 1-877-742-7822 to disable Passcode on all your devices. This will prevent others from attempting to log into your account with your Passcode.
  • What happens if I forget my Passcode?
    On the Passcode login screen, you can select "Forgot Passcode." You will then be prompted to log in with your User ID and Password and create a new Passcode.
  • Can I still log in with my User ID and Password?
    Yes, you can log in with your User ID and Password at any time by clicking on "User ID Login" from the login page.
  • What happens if I'm trying to log in with Passcode and enter it incorrectly too many times?
    For security purposes, you will be locked out of the application after 3 failed attempts. You can call 1-877-742-7822 to talk to a customer service representative about unlocking your account.
