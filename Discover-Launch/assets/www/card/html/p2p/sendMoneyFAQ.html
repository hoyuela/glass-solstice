Send Money

  • How does sending money with Discover Money Messenger work?
    You can send money to anyone with an email address or mobile phone number using Discover.com on your computer or mobile device. PayPal sends a message to your recipient letting them know their money is ready. Your recipient signs into their PayPal account to receive the money. If they don't have a PayPal account, all they need to do is sign up. The amount you send is charged to your Discover card account just like a purchase.
  • Are there any fees to send money through Discover Money Messenger?
    There are no fees to send money and the amount will be charged to your Discover card account like a purchase. If you are sending money to friends or family, there are no fees charged to them to accept the money. If you are sending money to a business for goods or services, PayPal will charge them a fee to accept the money.
  • Do I need a PayPal account to send money?
    No, you do not need a PayPal account to send money.
  • Does the person I'm sending money to need to be a Discover Cardmember?
    No, the person receiving the money doesn't need to be a Discover Cardmember. However, they will need to sign up for a PayPal account (if they don't already have one) in order to access their money.
  • Does the person I'm sending money to need a PayPal account?
    You can send money to anyone even if they don't yet have a PayPal account. But, the recipient must sign up for an account at PayPal.com in order to claim their money. When PayPal notifies the recipient that you have sent them money, they will also receive simple instructions on how to open up a PayPal account to access it.
  • What is the maximum amount I can send?
    You can send up to per day and up to per month, depending on your available credit limit.
  • Will I earn rewards on the transaction?
    Yes, you will earn rewards on this transaction just like any purchase.
  • How does the recipient actually get the money?
    If they are a registered PayPal account holder, the money is instantly deposited into their account. If the recipient is new to PayPal, they simply open an account, link their email address or mobile number and they will have immediate access to the money. After they log in to PayPal, they have the option to spend the money online at any merchant that accepts PayPal, transfer it to a bank account, or request a check.
  • How long does it take for the recipient to get the money?
    Money Messenger payments are credited to existing PayPal accounts immediately. If no PayPal account exists, the money is available as soon as the recipient creates an account or links their email address or mobile phone number to an existing account.
  • Is the recipient charged a fee?
    The recipient will be charged a fee by PayPal if you select "Pay for goods or services" or if they convert the money to another currency. PayPal fees are detailed in their User Agreement on PayPal.com.
  • How do I check the status of a transaction?
    You will receive a confirmation e-mail when the funds have been claimed. You can also check the status of any transaction in your Money Messenger Transaction History.
  • Can I cancel a payment I have already sent?
    If the transaction is still pending, you will be able to cancel the transaction from your Money Messenger Transaction History and you will be refunded the full amount.
  • What happens if I send money but it's not claimed?
    If the money isn't claimed within 30 days, you will be notified via e-mail and the full amount will be returned to your Discover card account. During this time you can also cancel the transaction from your Money Messenger Transaction History.
  • Where will the transaction show on my statement?
    The charge will post to your statement under the Services category and will be subject to the purchase APR on your account.
  • What APR will Money Messenger transactions receive?
    Money Messenger transactions will be subject to the purchase APR on your account.
  • Can I use this service to pay myself?
    No, you cannot send money to yourself.
  • As a Sender, will these transactions show in my PayPal account?
    If you have a PayPal account, Money Messenger transactions you send will not show in your PayPal account history. They are only tied to your Discover card account and will show in your Money Messenger Transaction History. within Discover.com.
  • Can I transfer money internationally?
    Yes. Please visit PayPal.com to learn more about which countries can receive money through an email address.
