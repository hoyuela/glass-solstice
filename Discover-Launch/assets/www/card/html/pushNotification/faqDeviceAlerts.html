Text and Push Alerts FAQs

  • What is a Push Alert?
    A Push Alert is a message sent from your Discover Mobile App on Android, iPhone and iPad devices, that informs you when new account information is available. Depending on your device settings, these alerts can display in the form of badges, banners or alerts, and be viewed again later on the Push Alerts History page. You will only receive alerts for which you have enrolled. These subscriptions can be updated through the Manage Text and Push Alerts page on the App.
  • How do I turn on/off Push Alerts?
    Here are the ways to control the number of Push Alerts that you receive.
    • From the Manage Text and Push Alerts page on the App, select the Push Alerts you would like to activate/deactivate then save your changes.
    • To turn on/off all Push Alerts, toggle the “Enable Push Alerts” switch from ON to OFF and vice versa, on the Manage Text and Push Alerts page on the App. Then save your changes.
    • Push Alerts can also be turned on/off via the mobile device settings. To turn on/off notifications on the device, navigate to the notification center in the device settings and locate the Discover App icon. After clicking on the icon, a screen will open from which you can turn off/on Push Alerts for the Discover App.
    Note: If you disable Discover Push Alerts from the device settings you will not be able to reach your Push Alerts settings in the Discover Mobile App until you turn the Discover Push Alerts back on in the device settings
  • I activated Push Alerts but I have not received any yet.
    First check to make sure that Discover Push Alerts are enabled in your mobile device settings. To turn on/off notifications on the device, navigate to the notification center in the handset settings and locate the Discover App icon. After clicking on the icon, a screen will open from which you can turn on/off Push Alerts for the Discover App.

    Also, Push Alerts require an internet connection to work. Please check to make sure that you are connected to the internet either through cellular data or through Wi-Fi. If you are connected and can log into the Discover Mobile App, check to make sure that your Push Alert subscriptions were saved on the Manage Text and Push Alerts page on the App.
  • What is the difference between Text and Push Alerts?
    Text Messages are messages that will be delivered to your phone from the Discover number (347268) and will count against the text message plan you have with your carrier. Please consult your carrier for details on your text message plan, including any fees that may apply.

    Push alerts (available via the Discover Mobile iPhone, iPad, and Android apps) will be sent through the application itself over a data connection. These messages do not count against your text message data plan with your carrier.

    Text Messages are available for all devices with text message receiving capabilities, while push alerts require the Discover Mobile application to be downloaded to the device.
  • How do I enroll in Text Messages?
    Go to the Manage Text and Push Alerts page on the App and select the Text Messages that you would like to receive. After you have selected your alerts, verify or provide your mobile phone number and carrier information. Save your settings. You can also manage your Text Message enrollments online at Discover.com.

    Shortly after saving you will receive an Opt-In text message from DISCOV (347268). An Opt-In message is a text message that is sent to the mobile number you provided to confirm your desire to receive text messages at the mobile number provided. You must text back “YES” along with the last 4 of your account number to complete your enrollment.
  • How much does it cost to receive a Text Message?
    Text charges vary by carrier and plan. Please contact your service provider to find out if your plan has fees for receiving text messages.
  • What Text Messages will Discover send me?
    You will only receive Text Messages for which you have enrolled. You can update your Text Messages subscriptions through the Manage Text and Push Alerts page on the App. You can also manage your Text Messages online at Discover.com.
  • How do I stop receiving Text Messages from Discover?
    Here are the ways to control the number of Text Messages that you receive.
    • From the Manage Text and Push Alerts page on the App, select the Text Messages you would like to activate/deactivate and then save your changes.
    • To unsubscribe from all Text Messages, text “STOPALL” to DISCOV (347268)
    • Update your Text Message enrollments on Discover.com
  • I activated Text Messages but I am not receiving any texts.
    Run through the following checklist to make sure your messages are set up correctly:
    • Confirm that the correct mobile number and carrier are entered on the Manage Text and Push Alerts page on the App
    • Make sure you have selected the Text Messages you would like to receive on the Manage Text and Push Alerts page on the App. Then send “YES” along with the last 4 of your account number to DISCOV (347268) to ensure that you are opted-in
    • Call 1-800-DISCOVER if the above conditions have been met and you are still not receiving Text Messages.
    • Please contact your mobile carrier if you are unable to receive Text Messages after attempting the steps above.
