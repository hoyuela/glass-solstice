Text and Push Alerts FAQs

  • What is a Push Alert?
    A Push Alert is a message sent from your Discover Mobile App on Android, iPhone and iPad devices, that informs you when new account information is available. Depending on your device settings, these alerts can display in the form of badges, banners or alerts, and be viewed again later on the Push Alerts History page. You will only receive alerts for which you have enrolled. These subscriptions can be updated through the Manage Text and Push Alerts page on the App. No fees are associated with Push Alerts.
  • How do I turn on/off Push Alerts?
    Below are the ways to manage your Push Alerts:
    • From the left-hand navigation in the Discover App, under Profile & Settings, select Manage Text and Push Alerts Then select the Push Alerts you would like to activate/deactivate and save your changes.
    • To turn on/off all Push Alerts, slide the "Enable Push Alerts" switch from ON to OFF and vice versa, on the Manage Text and Push Alerts page and save your changes.
    • Push Alerts also can be turned off/on via your mobile device settings. To turn on/off notifications on your device, go to the notification center in the device settings and locate the Discover Mobile App icon. Click the icon and a screen will open. Choose the option to turn off/on Push Alerts.
    Note: If you disable Discover Push Alerts from your mobile device settings you will not be able to change your Push Alerts settings in the Discover Mobile App until you turn the Discover Push Alerts back on in device settings.
  • I activated Push Alerts. Why have I not received any?
    Check to make sure Discover Push Alerts are enabled in your mobile device settings. To turn on notifications on your mobile device, go to the notification center in the device settings and locate the Discover Mobile App icon. Click the icon and a screen will open. Choose the option to turn on Push Alerts.
  • What is the difference between Text Alerts and Push Alerts?
    Text Messages are available for all devices with text message receiving capabilities while Push Alerts are sent directly through the Discover Mobile App.

    Note: Text charges vary by carrier and plan. Please contact your service provider to find out if your plan has fees for receiving text messages.
  • How do I enroll in Text Messages?
    To enroll in text messages, from the left-hand navigation in the Discover App, under Profile & Settings, select Manage Text and Push Alerts and choose the text message you would like to receive. Once you have chosen your alerts, you will be asked to verify or provide your mobile phone number and carrier information. Enter or verify the information and then save your settings. You will then receive an opt-in text message from "DISCOV (347268)." You must text back "YES" along with the last 4 digits of your account to complete your enrollment.

    You can also enroll in text messages on Discover.com under Account Profile and Preferences.
  • What Text Messages will Discover send me?
    You will only receive text messages for which you have enrolled. You can elect to enroll in a number of text message reminders including when statements are available, when your payment has posted, and when your payment is due.
  • Can I receive fraud alerts via Text Message?
    Yes. You can sign up to receive fraud alerts that will notify you when unusual activity is suspected in your account. To enroll, select Manage Text and Push Alerts under Profile & Settings, from the left-hand navigation in the Discover app and select the box next to "Fraud Alert."

    You can also sign up for this alert on discover.com under Account Profile and Preferences.
  • How do I stop receiving Text Messages from Discover?
    Below are the ways to manage your Text Alerts:
    • On the Manage Text and Push Alerts page in the Discover App, select the text messages you would like to activate/deactivate and save your changes.
    • To unsubscribe from all text messages, text "STOP ALL" to DISCOV (347268).
    • Update your text message enrollments on Discover.com via your Account Profile.
  • I signed up to receive several text alerts. Why have I not received any?
    You may need to confirm the mobile number and carrier are entered correctly on the Manage Text and Push Alerts page in the Discover App or under Account Profile and Preferences on Discover.com. You should also check to see if you have selected the text messages you would like to receive from the Manage Text and Push Alerts page and resend "YES" along with the last 4 digits of your accounts to DISCOV (347268) to ensure you are opted in for the alerts. If the above options still don't work, please call Discover web support at 1-877-742-7822. or contact your mobile carrier to make sure you are able to receive text messages.
  • Can I contact Discover Card via text message?
    Discover does not provide two-way communication with its customers through text messaging at this time. If you would like to stop receiving reminders, reply "STOP" from the most recent text message reminder you received. If you still continue to receive text messages after your have replied, please contact Discover web support at 1-877-742-7822.
  • Are text message reminders secure?
    We will never include your password or full account information in the message.
