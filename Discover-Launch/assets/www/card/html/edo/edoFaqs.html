EXTRAS FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions
  • What is Discover Extras?
    Discover Extras is another way you can save money on purchases at your favorite merchants. When you take advantage of an Extras offer, many of these will result in a statement credit on your Discover account while some offers may be instant savings. If you are offered instant savings, you will simply have to show the offer when making your purchase to receive your savings. Please be sure to read your offer details to see how to get your savings.
  • How do Discover Extras work?
    Many Discover Extras are offers that may result in statement credits for purchases you make at merchants you frequently shop at. If you have an offer you’d like to take advantage of, please check the offer details to see how your savings will work. If your offer is a statement credit, use your Discover Card within the specified dates and your account will be credited within 14 days. If your offer is a coupon, show the coupon when making your purchase to get your savings.
  • Do Discover Extras expire?
    Yes. All offers will eventually expire.
  • What will the statement credit look like on my statement?
    On your statement it will say: Discover Extra at (merchant).
  • Am I charged a fee if I receive a Discover Extra?
    No. There are no additional or hidden fees associated with receiving or using a Discover Extra.
  • What do I do if I have questions about my offers?
    If you need immediate assistance please call us at 1-800-347-2683.
  • What do the different colored indicators signify?
    The Red indicator means that you have three days or less to redeem your Extra offer. Yellow represents four to 10 days before your order will expire. Green shows that you have more than 10 days before your Extra offer will expire.
  • What merchant locations does the map show?
    The map will show merchant locations within your 15 mile radius or a 15 mile radius of the city or zip you are searching. If there are no merchant locations in your search area, your map will not show any results.
