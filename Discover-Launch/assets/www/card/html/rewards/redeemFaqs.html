Cashback Bonus FAQs

REDEMPTION

  • How do I start redeeming ?
    From the left-hand navigation, under Redeem Cashback Bonus, choose your redemption option.
  • How much of my Cashback bonus can I redeem ?
    Options starting at $20:
    • Partner gift cards and eCertificates from over 150 brand-name partners
    • Discover gift cards
    Options starting at $50:
    • Electronic deposit to your bank account
    • Statement credit for your Discover card account
    Note: You cannot redeem your Cashback Bonus if your account is delinquent or closed for any reason.
  • How long will it take to receive my rewards after I submit my order ?
    The time it takes to receive your reward varies based on your redemption:
    • Discover and Partner Gift Cards will be mailed to your billing address within 10 business days
    • Electronic deposits are sent to your bank account within 72 hours, however, banks differ and Discover cannot predict exactly how long before it will appear
    • Statement credits will post to your account within 72 hours
  • Do I have to redeem all of my Cashback bonus one time?
    No, you do not have to redeem all of your Cashback Bonus at one time. You can choose to redeem some or all of your Cashback Bonus –minimum redemption amounts vary by option.
  • What are eCertificates?
    eCertificates are a way to get rewards faster instead of waiting to receive a Partner Gift Card by mail. eCertificates are available immediately upon completing your order, and allow you to shop instantly with the Partner. Note: Please be aware that all Partners do not offer eCertificates. If the Partner does offer an eCertificate, you must select the eCertificate option in order to receive it.
  • Can I have Partner gift cards and Discover gift cards mailed to an address other than my billing address?
    No. As a security measure, we can only your mail gift cards to your billing addess.
  • Does my Cashback Bonus ever expire if I don't redeem it?
    Cashback Bonus rewards do not expire but may be forfeited if for any reason your account is closed, your account is inactive for 18 consecutive months or if you fail to make the minimum payment due by the payment due date for two consecutive billing periods.
  • Can I cancel an order I already placed?
    No. Once an order has been processed, it cannot be canceled. If you have questions about a processed order, please call 1-800-DISCOVER (1-800-347-2683) to contact Customer Service.
  • My statement credit hasn't been updated yet. Why is that?
    Statement credits may take up to 72 hours to post to your account.
  • Is my direct deposit available immediately once the transaction is processed?
    Direct deposits are sent to your bank account within 72 hours, however banks differ and Discover cannot predict exactly how long before it will appear.
  • What happens if I get logged out of the application before completing my order?
    You will have to start your order over if you get logged out of the application.
  • How do I confirm that my order has been submitted?
    Check Redemption History under Redeem Cashback Bonus, from the left-hand navigation for the status of your redemption. If Discover has your email on file, you will also receive an email confirming your order was submitted.
  • What should I do if I haven't received my reward?
    If it has been longer than 8 weeks since you submitted your order, please call 1-800-DISCOVER (1-800-347-2683) and a Customer Service Representative will assist you.
  • What are Partner gift cards?
    You can redeem your Cashback Bonus for physical gift cards from over 150 brand-name partners. To browse our Partner Gift Cards go to Partner Gift Cards & eCerts, under Redeem Cashback Bonus from the left-hand navigation. Once you are on the redemption page, select "Browse all Partners." Some Partner Gift Cards may provide additional value beyond your Cashback Bonus.
  • Where can I use Discover gift cards?
    Discover gift cards can be used wherever Discover card is accepted.
  • Are there certain states where I cannot redeem for a Discover Gift Card?
    Cardmembers who live in the following states cannot redeem for Discover Gift Cards: Colorado, Connecticut, Hawaii, Maine, New Hampshire, New Jersey, Rhode Island, Tennessee, and Vermont.

REFER A FRIEND

  • What's the Refer A Friend program?
  • How can I refer a friend?
    From the left hand navigation, under Earn Cashback Bonus or Earn Miles, select Refer a Friend. You can refer friends via email, Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn. We'll handle everything, starting by sending a unique link to your friend so we can track the status of your referral.
  • What should I include in my referral?
  • How many friends can I refer?
    You will only earn rewards for the first 10 friends approved for a Discover card per calendar year; however, there is no limit to how many friends you can refer.
  • How much can I earn with the Refer A Friend program?
  • To view your Refer a Friend status, select Refer a Friend from the left-hand navigation under Earn Cashback Bonus or "Earn Miles" in the Discover App.
