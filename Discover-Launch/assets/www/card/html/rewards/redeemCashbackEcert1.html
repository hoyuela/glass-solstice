  • How do eCertificates work?

    eCertificates are a way to get your gift card instantly instead of waiting to receive it by mail or email. eCertificates are available for use immediately in store or online (depending on the Partner). Upon completeing your redemption, a code will appear on your mobile device. You may use it immediately or retrieve it later within your Redemption History. To use the eCertificate, enter the code online or bring the code to the cashier at checkout. The cashier will enter the code as if it were a gift card.
