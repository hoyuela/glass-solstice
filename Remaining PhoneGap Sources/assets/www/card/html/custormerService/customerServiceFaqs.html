Frequently Asked Questions

    Security

  1. Is Discover.com Mobile as secure as Discover.com?
    Discover.com Mobile uses the same security standards as Discover.com.We ensure a high security level by never showing your account number, timing out each session afeter a period of inactivity, and by using your existing User Id and Password, which only you know.

    2. Log In Issues

  2. What do I need to log in to Discover.com Mobile?
    To log in, use the same User Id and Password that you have set up at Discover.com.

    3. Payments

  3. How do I change accounts?
    To update your bank information, log in to Discover.com and access the Payments tab.

    4. Transactions

  4. What if I want to see more than the last 5 payments?
    To see the last 7 months of payments, log in to Discover.com and access the Payments tab.
  5. How is the payment posting date selected?
    The payment posting date is the next available date.If you would like to change your payment post date, log in to Discover.com
  6. What if I want to see more than the last 20 transactions?
    To see up to 24 months of transactions, log in to Discover.com and click on the Recent activity and Statement links.
  7. Where is my Discover card accepted outside the U.S.?
    Cards are currently accepted in Canada, Central America, Mexico, the Caribbean and China.You can use your card everywhere Discover cards are accepted.

    8. International Usage

  8. How do I contact the card service center when traveling outside of the U.S.?
    We are open 24 hours, 7 days a week.While outside of the U.S., you can reach us by calling 001 plus 801 902 3100.
  9. What should I do if my card is lost or stolen while traveling abroad?
    If your card is lost or stolen while traveling outside of the U.S., immediately contact the Customer Service Center in the U.S. by dialing 001 plus 801-902-3100
