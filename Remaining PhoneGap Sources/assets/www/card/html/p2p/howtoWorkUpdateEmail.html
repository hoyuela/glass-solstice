Get Started

The fast,free way to send money securely to anyone.

  • All you need to send money is the recipient's e-mail address or mobile phone number.
  • The money is instantly available to your recipient and they are notified by e-mail or text message
  • The transaction will show up on your statement just like any other purchase.
  • You'll earn Cashback Bonus or Miles on this transaction.
  • You can send up to $200 a day, and up to $500 per month.

Here's how it works

  1. Simply enter your recipient's e-mail address or mobile number, choose an amount and send
  2. An e-mail or text message is sent to your recipient letting them know their money is ready.
  3. Your recipient logs in, or signs up for a Paypal account to retrieve the payment.
